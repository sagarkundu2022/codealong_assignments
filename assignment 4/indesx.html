The identity of Gumnami Baba is shrouded in as much mystery as the death of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose. Following the disappearance of the Indian nationalist after his overloaded Japanese plane crashed in Japanese-occupied Formosa (Taiwan), his followers continued to believe that he was alive. The conspiracy theories that refused to die down were further fanned by the presence of Gumnami Baba, a sadhu, who many thought was Netaji. Later, the UP government set up a one-man commission to unearth the identity of Gumnami Baba. While several films have been attempted on the life of Netaji, Tollywood will now churn out one on Gumnami Baba.
